Getting Started

My Diary is an AI-powered journaling platform designed to help you capture your thoughts, track your emotional journey, and gain valuable insights into your personal growth. Here's how it works:

  • Write Entries: Create daily journal entries with our rich text editor
  • Track Moods: Record your emotional state and track patterns over time
  • Get AI Insights: Receive personalized analysis and recommendations
  • Organize Content: Use categories, tags, and favorites to organize your thoughts
  • Export Data: Download your journal in various formats anytime

Our AI analyzes your entries to provide mood insights, writing suggestions, and personalized wellness recommendations.

Creating your first entry is simple:

  1. Sign up for a free account or log in
  2. Click the "New Entry" button in your dashboard
  3. Add a title for your entry
  4. Write your thoughts in the rich text editor
  5. Optionally set your mood, category, and tags
  6. Click "Save Entry" to store your thoughts

Pro tip: Start with just 5 minutes of writing about your day. Consistency is more important than length!

Privacy & Security

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Your entries are encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Private by Default: Only you can access your journal entries
  • No Data Mining: We never sell or share your personal content
  • Secure Servers: Enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • GDPR Compliant: Full compliance with data protection regulations

Our team cannot view your private journal entries. Your thoughts remain yours alone.

Our AI analysis is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Processing: Most analysis happens on your device when possible
  • Anonymized Data: Only anonymized patterns are used for improvement
  • No Personal Content: Your actual journal text is never shared with third parties
  • Optional Features: You can disable AI features if you prefer

The AI analyzes patterns in mood, writing style, and frequency to provide insights - but never shares your personal thoughts.

Troubleshooting

Resetting your password is easy:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a reset link (check spam folder too)
  5. Click the link and create a new password
  6. Log in with your new password

Note: Reset links expire after 24 hours for security.

AI insights require a minimum amount of content:

  • Write at least 3-5 entries to start seeing patterns
  • Each entry should be substantial (100+ words recommended)
  • Make sure you've set your mood for each entry
  • Check that AI features are enabled in your settings

Insights improve over time as the AI learns more about your writing patterns.

Journaling Tips

The ideal frequency depends on your goals:

  • Daily: Best for habit building and mood tracking
  • 3-4 times per week: Good balance for most people
  • Weekly: Minimum for meaningful insights

Key tip: Consistency matters more than length. Even 5 minutes daily is better than one long session weekly.

Write about whatever feels authentic to you:

  • Daily Events: What happened today and how you felt
  • Goals & Dreams: Your aspirations and progress
  • Challenges: Obstacles you're facing and solutions
  • Gratitude: Things you're thankful for
  • Self-Reflection: Thoughts about yourself and growth
  • Creative Ideas: Inspiration and brainstorming

Don't worry about being profound - honest, authentic writing is most valuable.

Build the habit with these strategies:

  • Start Small: Begin with just 2-3 minutes daily
  • Link to Existing Habits: Write after your morning coffee or before bed
  • Set Reminders: Use our app notifications or calendar alerts
  • Create a Ritual: Same time, same place, same routine
  • Don't Break the Chain: Use a calendar to track streaks
  • Be Forgiving: If you miss a day, just start again tomorrow

Habit formation takes about 21 days - stick with it!
